Task 7.4

Registration Cards

Cards for displaying user's event registrations. Two variants: Upcoming registrations (with status badge and cancel option) and Completed registrations (with review CTA).

Design Specifications:
Upcoming Registration: Event thumbnail, title, date, status badge (Pending/Approved/Confirmed), View & Cancel actions
Completed Registration: Event thumbnail, title, completed date, "Write a Review" CTA (if not reviewed), "View Review" (if reviewed)
Files: registered-event-card.tsx, completed-event-card.tsx
Option A: Vertical Card Layout RECOMMENDED
Standard vertical card with 16:9 image, clear status badge, and full-width action buttons. Consistent with event card design. Good visual hierarchy.
