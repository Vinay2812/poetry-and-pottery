Care Guide

Keep your handcrafted pottery looking beautiful

💧

Hand Wash Only

Gently wash with mild soap and warm water. Avoid abrasive scrubbers.

⚠

Avoid Extreme Heat

Not suitable for oven, stovetop, or open flame use.

❄

No Freezer

Avoid putting pottery in the freezer as it may crack.

🔥

Microwave Safe

Safe for short microwave use (under 2 minutes).

💡

Pro Tips

  • For stubborn stains, soak in warm water with baking soda
  • Dry thoroughly before storing to prevent moisture damage
  • Store with soft padding between stacked pieces

No Freezer

🔥

Microwave Safe

Safe for short microwave use.

💡

Pro Tips for Long-Lasting Pottery

  • For stubborn stains, soak in warm water with a teaspoon of baking soda for 30 minutes
  • Always dry thoroughly before storing to prevent moisture damage
  • Store with soft padding or felt between stacked pieces
  • Avoid sudden temperature changes to prevent thermal shock
