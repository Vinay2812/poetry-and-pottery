Task 8.2: Order Progress Stepper

Visual indicator for order status progression. Shows completed steps, current status, and upcoming steps with connected lines and icons.

Awaiting Approval

CURRENT BEHAVIOR

  • Simple text-based status
  • No visual progression
  • Hard to understand timeline
  • No date/time information

NEW DESIGN

  • Visual stepper with circles and lines
  • Clear completed/active/upcoming states
  • Icons for each step (optional)
  • Date/time below each step
  • Horizontal for desktop, vertical for mobile
  • Dashed lines for upcoming steps
