Implementation Notes -- Option 4: Visual Journey / Timeline Strip

Concept: Story-driven timeline approach presenting pricing tiers as milestones on a journey. Emphasizes skill progression and the creative experience rather than just prices.
Location: Homepage section, replaces/enhances existing workshops area.
Hero Section: Cream-to-white gradient background (#F5F0E8 to #FAFAF9), "Daily Workshops" title, subtitle about the journey, and a terracotta-colored tagline pill.
Timeline Milestones (5 stages):
  1h "Learn the Basics" -- ₹950/pp, 1 piece (centering clay, basic shapes)
  2h "Shape Your Vision" -- ₹1,600/pp, 2 pieces (throwing forms, pulling walls)
  3h "Refine & Detail" -- ₹2,200/pp, 3 pieces (trimming, handles, textures)
  5h "Master the Craft" -- ₹3,400/pp, 5 pieces (multiple pieces, advanced techniques)
  10h "Full Immersion" -- ₹7,500/pp, 10 pieces (complete pottery journey) [FEATURED]
Connecting Line: Gradient from green (#E8ECE8) through primary (#4F6F52) to terracotta (#C4785A) -- visually represents progression.
Icons: Each milestone has a pottery-themed icon in a circle with an hour badge overlay.
What's Included (4 items): All Materials, Expert Instruction, Kiln Firing, Take Home -- each with icon, label, and short description.
Schedule: "Open daily, 1 PM - 7 PM IST" in a warm cream pill.
CTA: "Start Your Pottery Journey" -- rounded-full green button with shadow, linking to /events.
Mobile: Vertical top-to-bottom timeline, each milestone as a card with node on left. Featured card uses primary bg. 2-column included grid.
Tablet: Horizontal scrollable timeline with scroll hint. 4-column included grid. Centered CTA.
Desktop: Full horizontal timeline visible at once. Hover animations on milestones. 4-column included grid. Centered layout.
Key Differentiator: Storytelling approach -- the timeline communicates "the more hours, the more you learn and create" naturally through visual progression rather than a static price list.
