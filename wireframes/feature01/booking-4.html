Option 4: Calendar-First with Inline Selection

A large monthly calendar is the dominant element. Each day cell shows small colored dots indicating slot availability. Clicking a day expands an inline panel with 6 hourly slots (1PM-7PM). Workshop config is selected via a floating dropdown. Selected slots appear as chips in a floating booking bar at the bottom.

Pattern: Calendar-dominant layout with inline slot expansion and floating booking bar
Day Dots: Green = available, Gray = full, Red = blocked (visible on each day cell)
Pricing: 1 hr = ₹950, 2 hr = ₹1,600, 10 hr = ₹7,500 (bulk pack)
Time Slots: 1 PM – 7 PM IST (6 hourly slots)
Slot States: Available (white/green border), Selected (green fill), Full (gray), Blocked (striped red)
Booking Bar: Shows selected slot chips, total hours, price preview, and "Continue to Book" button

Calendar-First Design — Design Notes:
Calendar Dominance: The monthly calendar is the primary UI element; all other elements (config, slots, booking bar) are secondary and contextual
Day Cell Dots: Small colored dots (green = available, gray = full, red = blocked) give at-a-glance availability for each day
Desktop Hover: Hovering over a day cell shows a tooltip with slot count breakdown (e.g., "3 available, 2 full, 1 blocked")
Slot Expansion: Mobile = bottom sheet; Tablet = side panel (320px); Desktop = inline panel below calendar (connected section)
Config Selector: Floating dropdown above calendar; compact on mobile, shows pricing tiers on tablet/desktop
Booking Bar: Shows selected slot chips (like a cart), total hours, price preview, and "Continue to Book" CTA. Mobile: stacked layout. Desktop: horizontal strip
Pricing: ₹950/hr (shown as ₹1,600 for 2 selected hours); bulk pack ₹7,500/10hr
Slot States: Available (white + green border), Selected (green fill), Full (gray, disabled), Blocked (red stripe, disabled)
