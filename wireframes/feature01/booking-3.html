Stepper/Wizard Flow — Design Notes:
Stepper Progression: Green filled circle + checkmark = completed step; Outlined circle with shadow = current active step; Gray circle = upcoming step
Mobile: Compact dots (no labels) to save space; step number shown as "Step X of 3" text
Tablet: Full stepper with labels; content has generous 40px horizontal padding
Desktop: Wizard card capped at max-width 800px; elegant stepper with wider connector lines
Slot States: White/green border = Available; Green fill = Selected; Gray bg = Full; Red striped = Blocked
Pricing: ₹950/hr, ₹1,600/2hr, ₹7,500/10hr pack; savings callout encourages bulk booking
Navigation: Back button (text) on left, Next/Confirm (primary pill) on right; Step 1 has no Back button
Interactions: Clicking a completed step circle navigates back to that step; config cards have hover + selected states
