Implementation Notes (Option 3):
Location: Homepage section, replaces/enhances existing workshops area.
Approach: Interactive pricing calculator with slider control. Dynamic pricing updates as user moves the slider between hour values.
Slider: Horizontal track with markers at 1, 2, 3, 5, and 10 hours. Active/passed markers highlighted in green. Draggable thumb with shadow.
Dynamic Display: Selected hours (large), price (bold), per-person text, pottery piece icons (scale with hours), detail chips (pieces count + per-hour rate), savings indicator.
Savings Calculation: Shows difference vs buying individual 1-hour sessions. E.g., 3hrs: ₹2,850 (3x₹950) - ₹2,200 = ₹650 saved.
Desktop Breakdown: Desktop pricing card includes a line-item breakdown: original price (strikethrough), package discount, total.
Popular Combinations (3 cards):
  "Quick Session" -- 1hr, ₹950, 1 piece, perfect for beginners.
  "Half Day" -- 3hr, ₹2,200, 3 pieces, most popular (green badge).
  "Full Experience" -- 5hr, ₹3,400, 5 pieces, best value per hour (terracotta badge).
Workshop Details: Timings (1-7 PM IST), Skill Level (All levels), Capacity (Max 6), Availability (7 days).
CTA: Dynamic text: "Book [X]-Hour Workshop" linking to /events. Updates with slider selection.
Mobile: Full-width slider, stacked pricing, horizontal combo cards.
Tablet: Slider + pricing side by side, 3-column combo cards.
Desktop: Two-column (slider left, sticky pricing card right), full-width combo cards below.
