TASK 6.4

Address Components

Address card, selector grid, and form designs for checkout and account pages.
Includes selection states, edit/delete actions, and "Add New" functionality.

In Progress
Current vs. Proposed Changes

Current Issues

  • Address cards have inconsistent styling
  • Selection state not visually clear
  • Add new address action not prominent
  • Edit/delete actions hard to discover
  • Form inputs lack consistent styling

Proposed Design

  • Consistent card styling with bg-neutral-50 or white
  • Clear selection with ring-2 ring-primary
  • Prominent "Add New" card with dashed border
  • Subtle icon buttons (top-right) for actions
  • Clean form inputs with rounded-lg styling
