Notes / Feedback
Implementation Specs (Customize Step 3 - Review & Summary):

• Context: User has completed Step 1 (Category) and Step 2 (Options), now reviews before adding to cart
• Step Indicator: Shows 3 steps with completed checkmarks for steps 1-2, active state for step 3
• Preview Card: Shows selected category image with "Custom" badge overlay
• Selections Display: Shows all selections with color swatches inline, custom text in italic
• Price Breakdown: Base price (normal), modifiers in green (+₹X), total bold
• Edit Links: Primary color, navigate back to Step 1 or Step 2 respectively
• Quantity Selector: Optional, pill-style +/- controls with min 1
• CTAs: Primary "Add to Cart" button, secondary "Wishlist" button or icon
• Mobile: Sticky footer with total + dual CTAs
• Animation: Heart icon animates on wishlist add
• Success: Redirect to cart or show success toast on add to cart
