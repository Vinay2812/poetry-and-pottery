Care Instructions

Keep your handcrafted pottery looking beautiful for years to come.

🧻

Hand Wash Only

Use mild soap and warm water. Avoid dishwasher for glazed pieces.

🌡️

Avoid Extreme Heat

Don't expose to sudden temperature changes. Let cool naturally.

❄️

No Freezer

Do not place pottery in the freezer. Moisture can cause cracking.

♨

Microwave Safe

Check the label. Most of our pieces are microwave safe for reheating.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Store pottery with felt or cloth between stacked pieces
  • Use a soft sponge — avoid abrasive scrubbers
  • Natural variations in glaze are part of the handcrafted charm
  • For stubborn stains, soak in warm water with baking soda

Care Instructions

Keep your handcrafted pottery looking beautiful for years to come.

🧻

Washing Your Pottery

Always hand wash your pottery with mild soap and warm water. Avoid placing handcrafted pieces in the dishwasher as the intense heat and harsh detergents can damage the glaze over time.

🌡️

Temperature Care

Avoid exposing your pottery to sudden temperature changes. Don't pour boiling water into a cold pot, and let heated pieces cool naturally before washing.

❄️

Storage Tips

Store your pottery in a dry place. When stacking pieces, place felt or soft cloth between them to prevent scratching. Avoid storing in freezing conditions.

♨

Microwave & Oven Use

Most of our pieces are microwave safe for reheating — check the label on the bottom of each piece. Not all pieces are oven safe; look for the oven-safe mark.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Store pottery with felt or cloth between stacked pieces
  • Use a soft sponge — avoid abrasive scrubbers
  • Natural variations in glaze are part of the handcrafted charm
