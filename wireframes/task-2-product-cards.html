Tasks 2.1-2.7: Add to Cart + Wishlist buttons always visible at the bottom of every card. 3:4 image ratio, no borders, display font for names, image zoom on hover (desktop).

Status: Implemented

BEFORE (Current)

  • Border + shadow + hover lift
  • 1:1 square image with carousel
  • Rounded-[2rem] (32px corners)

AFTER (New Design)
