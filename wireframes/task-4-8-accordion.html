Foundation component: Clean accordion without heavy borders. Smooth expand/collapse animation with rotating chevron indicator.

APPROVED — Option A: Clean Minimal

CURRENT BEHAVIOR

  • Heavy borders between items
  • No smooth animations
  • Inconsistent expand/collapse indicators
  • Mixed styling patterns

NEW DESIGN

  • Chevron: Rotate animation on expand
