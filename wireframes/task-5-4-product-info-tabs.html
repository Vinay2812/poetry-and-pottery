Approval Selection

Please select your preferred option for each viewport.

Option A (Recommended): Underlined Tabs - Classic style with primary color underline indicator. Clean, minimal, and familiar UX pattern.
Option B: Pill/Segment Tabs - Modern contained look with white active background. More playful and contemporary.
Option C: Full-Width Equal Tabs - Bold, prominent tabs that stretch to fill width. Works well on mobile but may feel heavy on desktop.
